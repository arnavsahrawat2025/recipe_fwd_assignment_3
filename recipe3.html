Berry Blast Smoothie

Prep Time: 5 Mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Mixed Berries
  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1/2 cup Milk

Step-by-Step

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a tall glass.
  4. Garnish with a few whole berries.
