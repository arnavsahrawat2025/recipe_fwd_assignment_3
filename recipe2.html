Margherita Pizza

Prep Time: 40 Mins

Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Fresh Basil Leaves

Step-by-Step

  1. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  2. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce.
  3. Add mozzarella slices and bake at 250°C.
  4. Top with fresh basil before serving.
