Classic Cheeseburger

Prep Time: 25 Mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Beef or Veggie Patty
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Burger Buns
  • Lettuce and Tomato

Step-by-Step

  1. Grill the patty until cooked through.
  2. Toast the buns on a pan.
  3. Place cheese on the patty to melt.
  4. Assemble with veggies and sauce.

Author: Arnav Sahrawat | SAU Assignment
