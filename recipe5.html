White Sauce Pasta

Prep Time: 25 Mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Penne Pasta
  • 2 tbsp Butter & Flour
  • 1 cup Milk
  • Parmesan Cheese

Step-by-Step

  1. Boil pasta until tender.
  2. Make a roux with butter and flour, then slowly whisk in milk.
  3. Stir until thick, then add cheese and seasonings.
  4. Toss pasta into the sauce and serve warm.
