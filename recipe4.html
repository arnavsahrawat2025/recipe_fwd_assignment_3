Pepper Crust Steak

Prep Time: 30 Mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 200g Ribeye or Sirloin
  • Crushed Black Peppercorns
  • Butter and Garlic
  • Sea Salt

Step-by-Step

  1. Season steak heavily with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear in a hot pan with oil for 3 minutes per side.
  3. Add butter and garlic, then baste the steak.
  4. Let the meat rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
